Deliveries/Returns

Where do you ship to?

We ship to mainland UK. Unfortunately, we do not ship internationally but we may do so soon!

Do you offer any refunds?

Yes, we can refund a product if you are not happy with it. Refunds will only be offered to products bought in the past 14 days.

Do you accept exchanges?

Currently, we do not offer exchanges once a product has been chosen.

Meet Ups

I would like to join, but I cannot find a time to suit me.

We are currently looking into adding more meet up times.

I don't see the game that I would like to play. What can I do?

We are currently looking into adding more games to our meet ups.

I can't make it for the whole evening. What can I do?

Donations/Board Game Swaps

Can I sell my board game?

Unfortunately, we currently do not buy board games. You may donate board games or make a swap.

I want to get rid of my board game but I cannot find a game that I would like to swap with. What can I do?

Our catalogue of board games change regularly so come back to find one that you are interested in.

The board game I recieved is not what I expected. What can I do?

We describe the conditions of our board games to the best of our ability. If it is not upto expectations, please call us on 0123 456 7890.
